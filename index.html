VI

The Weekly Market Run

The bases and pantries are done. Weekly, refresh produce, herbs, and greens.

Shop brightness, not recipes.

Leafy greens
A bunch or two every trip. Asian market — gai lan, bok choy, pea shoots, yu choy, long beans. Whole Foods — kale, chard, escarole, spinach, arugula.
Fresh herbs
Cilantro, basil, dill, mint, parsley, scallions.
Acid & crunch
Citrus, cucumber, radish, cabbage, in-season tomato.
One fresh protein
Salmon, eggs, or extra shrimp.

Asian and specialty markets handle shelf-stable pantry restocks occasionally — not weekly.
